ABC Principle Reference

Accuracy, Brevity, Clarity

Military slides must be concise. Remove unnecessary words:

  • Articles: a, an, the
  • Auxiliaries: is, are, has, have
  • Personal Pronouns: I, we, they
  • Long Connectors: "in order to" → "IOT", "due to the fact that" → "due to"
  • Passive Voice: "was reported" → "reported"
Example: "The enemy has been observed establishing new defensive positions" → "En: new def posns observed"
